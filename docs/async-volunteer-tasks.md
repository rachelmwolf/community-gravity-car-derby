# Async Volunteer Tasks

## Purpose

This document lists work that can be done outside race day by volunteers who only have small blocks of time to give.

## Good Async Work Principles

- can be completed in under 1 to 3 hours per session
- has a clear owner and output
- does not require being present for live race operations
- can be reviewed by a lead before it becomes final

## Planning And Communications

| Task | Output | Best Fit | Time Block |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-race email drafting | message drafts and schedule | clear writer, organized volunteer | 1 to 2 hours |
| FAQ cleanup | participant FAQ draft | detail-oriented editor | 1 hour |
| Resource reminder planning | checklist of links, docs, and reminders | organized planner | 1 hour |
| Committee follow-up notes | action summary after meetings | reliable note-taker | 30 to 60 minutes |

## Awards And Printables

| Task | Output | Best Fit | Time Block |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Award design selection | shortlist of trophy concepts | creative volunteer | 1 to 2 hours |
| 3D printing coordination | printer owner identified and schedule confirmed | organized coordinator | 30 to 60 minutes |
| Sizing-guide template design | printable dimension guides for check-in and inspection | crafty, detail-oriented volunteer | 1 to 2 hours |
| Print coordination | printed signs, guides, or forms ready for event week | reliable volunteer with printer access | 30 to 60 minutes |

## Venue And Materials Prep

| Task | Output | Best Fit | Time Block |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Signage prep | labeled signs ready for printing | organized, basic design comfort | 1 to 2 hours |
| Supply shopping list | complete purchase list with links | practical, detail-oriented volunteer | 30 to 60 minutes |
| Tool and station bin prep | packed inspection or setup bins | methodical volunteer | 1 to 2 hours |
| Chair and table labeling plan | simple setup instructions for volunteers | organized volunteer | 30 to 60 minutes |

## Community And Guest Experience

| Task | Output | Best Fit | Time Block |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Recruiting table prep | display plan, handouts, or sign-up prompts | outgoing, guest-focused volunteer | 1 hour |
| Design-award category suggestions | shortlist with plain-language descriptions | creative volunteer | 30 to 60 minutes |
| Welcome script review | concise greeting and direction script | guest-facing volunteer | 30 minutes |
| Family resource list | simple list of build resources and FAQs | organized helper | 30 to 60 minutes |

## Recommended First Async Tasks For This Event

- build the pre-race communication sequence
- choose award designs and identify a 3D printer owner
- create sizing-guide templates for fast check-in
- prepare event signage list and printing owner
- prepare recruiting table materials

## Assignment Rule

Any async task should have:

- one named owner
- one clear deliverable
- one due date
- one reviewer

If those are not clear, the task will usually stall.
