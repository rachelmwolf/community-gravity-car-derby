# Volunteer Role Catalog

## Purpose

This document turns the volunteer model into short, assignable roles. It is written for committee members who need to recruit quickly and for volunteers who need to decide where they fit.

## How To Use This

- assign critical leads first
- fill race-control support second
- fill guest-facing and optional roles after core operations are covered
- offer short-shift roles to volunteers with only 1 to 3 hours
- offer async roles to volunteers who cannot commit to race-day coverage

## Race-Day Support Roles

These roles are easier to break into short shifts.

| Role | What This Person Does | Best Fit | Typical Shift |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Check-In Support | greet racers, verify names, direct next steps | organized, friendly, quick with repeated tasks | 1 to 2 hours |
| Inspection Assistant | help measure cars and move through the checklist | detail-oriented, calm, process-reliable | 1 to 3 hours |
| Pit / Repair Support | help with small approved fixes | handy, patient, practical | 1 to 3 hours |
| Staging Runner | move accepted cars into race order | active, organized, repetitive-task tolerant | 1 to 2 hours |
| Finish / Retrieval | return cars and keep lanes clearing | active, dependable | 1 to 2 hours |
| Floater | fill gaps, cover breaks, solve small problems | flexible, observant | 1 to 3 hours |
| Setup / Teardown Crew | move tables, signs, chairs, and equipment | physically able, reliable | 1 to 4 hours |

## Guest-Facing Roles

These roles are good for volunteers who like people more than process control.

| Role | What This Person Does | Best Fit | Typical Shift |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Guest Experience Lead | own welcome tone, recruiting, and family-facing energy | outgoing, creative, warm | event morning through awards |
| Greeter / Welcome | direct families and answer basic questions | approachable, visible, friendly | 1 to 2 hours |
| Recruiting Support | staff table and talk with families | enthusiastic, conversational | 1 to 3 hours |
| Hospitality / Concessions | support snacks and comfort items | guest-facing, practical | 1 to 3 hours |
| Design-Award Support | help with display, excitement, and design recognition | creative, audience-friendly | 1 to 2 hours |
