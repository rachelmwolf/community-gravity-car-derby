# Day-Of Run Sheet

## Before Families Arrive

- volunteer check-in
- final room and track setup
- inspection station ready
- pit / repair area ready
- scoring and race software ready
- signage visible
- first-aid location confirmed

## Check-In Window

- greet families
- verify entrant information
- send each car to inspection
- route failed cars to pit / repair
- move passed cars to impound / staging

## Before Racing Starts

- close inspection line at defined time
- confirm staging order
- confirm MC opening notes
- confirm scoring is ready
- confirm safety lead is in place

## Racing

- keep heats moving
- keep accepted cars in controlled staging
- use floaters to cover bottlenecks
- announce what is happening often
- log any rule or safety issue

## Awards

- confirm final results
- prepare awards in final order
- run awards quickly and clearly

## Closeout

- teardown assignments
- equipment check
- incident notes
- collect lessons learned while they are fresh
